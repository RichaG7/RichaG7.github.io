Make a Sandwich!

  1. Get two pieces of bread
  2. Put the bread on a plate
  3. Smear butter
  4. Heat up pan
  5. Toast the bread in hot pan
  6. Put bread back on plate
  7. Smear peanut butter on one bread
  8. Smear your favorite jam on the other bread
  9. Put the breads together, smeared side in
